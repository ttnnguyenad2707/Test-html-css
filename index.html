Home - Shutters

At Empire, our shutters encapsulate quality, innovation and value. They are designed to capture the essence of contemporary and modern design without sacrificing functionality and value. Made with the highest quality and durable materials, Empire’s shutters can withstand the harshest of Australian climates, from roaring winds to blazing hot sun rays.

You can also maximise shade during the summer and keep warm during the winter with our latest insulation shutter technology, allowing you to save on your energy bills. These shutters are simple and convenient to use; with just a tilt of a rod, you can have full control of your privacy and how much light to allow in.

Our catalogue of shutters comprises a selection of timeless and sophisticatedly variety of shutters, such as Plantation shutters, Aluminium shutters, Timber shutters and Roller shutters.

Enhance your home's look and impress your guests with some of Empire’s sleek and sophisticated shutters. Custom-made and it comes in various colours and designs, Empire Window Furnishings will make sure you find the perfect match.
